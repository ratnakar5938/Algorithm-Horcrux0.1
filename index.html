About Clubs

Codered

CodeRed, the official competitive programming club of National Institute of Technology Rourkela, is an endeavour in the direction of refining the problem solving flair. We are a team of driven programmers who are intrigued by the wonder of competitive coding and take strides to foster the culture of the same. We believe in community building and enrich the same through the medium of workshops, contests, discussions etc. Ever since we started, we have  witnessed and cultivated growth in our community in this domain like never before.

Neuromancers

Neuromancers, the programming society of IIT Bhubaneswar, is a group of some of the most dedicated and talented programmers from the institute. Having a competitive evaluation and selection procedure, only a handful of the top candidates get inducted into the society. The society has members proficient in a plethora of fields, including but not limited to Cloud Computing, Android Development, Machine Learning, Web Development, and are dedicated to organising programming contests and workshops, and make coding a fun experience for everyone.

IIT-Bhubaneswar

Indian Institute of Technology Bhubaneswar, having been established in 2008, is one of the second generation IITs. The campus is spread across 936 acres, making it the 4th largest IIT, and lies on the outskirts of Bhubaneswar city. Resting at the foot of Barunei Hill, the campus boasts a scenic, lush green environment. The institute was recently awarded as “University of the Year” at the FICCI Higher Education Excellence Awards 2021 ceremony held at its 16th annual summit, and features within the top 12 IITs in the country.

NIt-rourkela

NIT Rourkela is one of the premier national level institutions for technical education in the country and is funded by the Government of India. Government of India has elevated the Regional Engineering College, Rourkela to a deemed university under the name of National Institute of Technology, Rourkela.The campus of the Institute consisting of the Institute buildings, halls of residence and staff colony is situated at the eastern end of Rourkela steel city, beyond Sector-1 over an area of 262 hectares of land provided by the Government of Orissa. It is a residential campus offering accommodation to faculty, staff and students. The campus has all the amenities for developing personal, social and academic skills of the student community.
